Scholorship News

PGC offers merit-based scholarships for students who show excellent academic performance. Criteria of Merit-based scholarship is the percentage of marks obtained by the applicant. Students with 75 % marks or above percent avail 100 % fee waive.

Our Notice Board

Students with 75% or above marks are eligible to apply for free education at the time of admission. Whereas, students with 70% or above marks in can apply for a special fee concession of 50% percentage.
